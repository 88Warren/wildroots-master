Welcome to Wild Roots Kitchen & Bar

Hi! We’re Wild Roots Kitchen and Bar and we provide awesome, ethical, planet-saving catering, right here in South Wales.

We’re passionate about supplying great tasting, locally sourced and sustainable food and drink, with an unparalleled level of service and always with a smile!

We deliver incredible cuisine for weddings, corporate dinners and private parties. We also offer a unique service, providing white-label catering for Welsh wedding venues and those in the south West of England, which we combine with a collaborative approach to reduce our collective carbon footprint.

Whether you work for us, or we work for you, we’re all one team! We love what we do and we’re obsessive about going above and beyond. You will never see us have a bad day at the office!

We’re Proud to Support

Size of Wales

We are delighted to work with Size of Wales. Through them, we plant a tree for every sale that we make to support a family or community that needs it most across the world.

We strive every day to reduce our impact on our natural world, in every decision that we make.

Although becoming carbon neutral solely through tree planting is not the endgame, it does at least help to mitigate some of our impact, whilst we tackle those areas currently out of our control.

Lllamau

We are very proud to support Llamau - the leading homelessness charity in Wales, supporting the most vulnerable young people and women.

They are particularly well known for working with those most at risk - care leavers, people who have been involved with the criminal justice system, people who have experienced domestic abuse and people who have had chaotic and disadvantaged lifestyles who need high levels of individual support to help them gain the skills necessary for them to live independent and purposeful lives in their communities.
